How Design Can Help Reduce Smoking Habits

Design Research / 2025

Role

Research, Data Analysis

Tools

Figma

We wanted to research on how design can be used as a tool to help reduce smoking habits among college students. Through a combination of quantitative and qualitative research methods, we aimed to uncover insights into the motivations behind smoking and identify opportunities for design interventions.

Download PDF

We utilized quantitative surveys (N=70) combined with qualitative user interviews to identify the primary pain points. The data revealed a significant gap between perceived addiction and actual behavior.
