Design Research

Methodology / 2025

Role

UX Research, Data Analysis

Tools

Figma, Miro, Python

Objective

Understanding user behavior patterns to inform architectural decision making in digital spaces.

Design is often seen as the final coat of paint, but the real work happens in the invisible architecture of information. This project documents the research phase for a financial literacy app.

Fig 1. User Journey & Empathy Mapping

We utilized quantitative surveys (N=500) combined with qualitative user interviews to identify the primary pain points. The data revealed a significant gap between perceived financial knowledge and actual behavior.

Archetypal User Personas developed from interview data.

Information Architecture

Before a single pixel was pushed, we structured the sitemap to prioritize accessibility. Card sorting exercises helped us organize the navigation into intuitive clusters.
