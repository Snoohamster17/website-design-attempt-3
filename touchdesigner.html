TouchDesigner

Concept

Exploring the boundary between organic noise and mathematical precision through node-based programming.

This collection documents experiments in real-time visual synthesis. The goal is to create systems that can react performatively to sound and user input, rather than static pre-rendered frames.

By manipulating feedback loops and TOP compositing, simple geometric primitives are transformed into complex, evolving textures.
