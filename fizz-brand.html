Story Book Illustration

Illustration / 2025

Role

Illustration, Layout Design, Storyboarding

Tools

Procreate, Illustrator, InDesign

Concept

Visual storytelling focused on whimsical characters and immersive environments, bringing the narrative to life through digital illustration.

This project explores the aboriginal art style for The Young King by Oscar Wilde. The goal was to create a visually appealing illustrated story book that draws inspiration from the Aborigini culture and integrate visually.

The illustrations utilize a vibrant color palette to engage the reader, while the typography is set to ensure readability across different age groups.
