75 Years of Gujarat

Identity Design / 2025

Role

Visual Identity, Logo Design

Context

State Anniversary Celebration

Concept

Incorporating Gujarat's vibrancy and rich heritage into the number 75 (७५).

The challenge was to create a commemorative logo that honored 75 years of statehood. Without official state colors, the design draws inspiration from the vibrancy of the state and its festivities.

Using Gestalt principles of Closure and Proximity to form the Gujarati numerals '75' (७५).

The visual language is supported by Anek Gujarati, a variable font chosen for its versatility across print and broadcast media. It offers a flexible system ranging from graphic textures to bold headlines.

Final Execution

Elements

Ashoka Pillars, Government Tagline

Variations

Full Color, Monochrome

Using references from other official stamps, the final logo emphasizes authority and celebration. The composition includes the Ashoka Pillars to symbolize nationalism, grounded by the "Gujarat State Government" title.
