PETRICHOR &

Magazine Design / 2024

Role

Visual Identity, Mockups

Tools

InDesign, Photoshop

"Petrichor &" is more than a magazine; it is an archive of my attempts at publication design. The design objective was to translate the ephemeral scent of rain into a tangible visual experience.

Editorial Spread Layout

The grid system was designed to be fluid, mimicking the unpredictability of weather patterns. We utilized negative space heavily to allow the content to "breathe," much like the earth after a fresh rain.

By combining hand-drawn elements in Procreate with rigorous InDesign layouts, the final publication bridges the gap between organic illustration and structured communication design.
