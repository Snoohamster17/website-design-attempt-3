Editorial Design / 2025

Role

Art Direction, Layout, Typography

Tools

InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator

Brief

Design a 100-page bi-annual publication focusing on sustainable architecture.

The challenge was to create a layout that felt open and breathable, reflecting the subject matter of sustainable architecture. We utilized a 12-column grid but frequently broke it to create tension and visual interest.

Fig 1. Cover Spread Design

Typography played a crucial role. We selected a high-contrast serif for headlines to evoke a sense of heritage, paired with a geometric sans-serif for body copy to maintain modernity.

The final result is a tactile experience that invites the reader to slow down and engage with the content.
